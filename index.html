Our Story

A Journey of Flavors

Nestled in the heart of the city, Aquarella brings the authentic flavors of Italy to your table. Our journey began in 2005 when Chef Marco Bianchi returned to his roots, combining generations of family recipes with contemporary culinary techniques.

We source only the finest ingredients, many imported directly from Italy, to create dishes that honor tradition while embracing innovation. Our pasta is handmade daily, our sauces simmered for hours, and our desserts crafted with passion.

At Aquarella, we believe dining is an experience to be savored - from the warm ambiance inspired by the Italian countryside to the carefully curated wine list featuring boutique vineyards.
